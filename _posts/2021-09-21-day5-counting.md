Day 5

Binomial Coefficients  
$${n \choose k}={n \choose n-k}$$  
$${n \choose k}={n-1 \choose k}+{n-1 \choose k-1}$$  
$$(a+b)^{n}={n \choose 0}a^{n}+{n \choose 1}a^{n-1}b+\cdots+{n \choose n}b^{n}$$  
$${n \choose 0}=1={n \choose n}$$  
$$2^{n}={n \choose 0}+\cdots+{n \choose n}$$
